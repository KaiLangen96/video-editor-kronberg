About Me

My name is Hanna Kronberg and I am a thorough and efficient, certified Avid Editor/Trailer producer.
I have worked at SVT and TV4 as a trailer producer, Appelberg as a content marketing editor for, among other things, Unicef, Atlas Copco, Scania etc.
Before that I worked at STRIX as Offline editor for SOS Sweden s.2 and Torpet.
I edit, among other things, interviews, trailers, reality, various types of short films, clips for social media and documentaries and I am very adaptable to the format offered.
I can also perform a bit audio processing and grading as well as light retouching on still photos.
